Spiritbox Fanpage

This webpage is all about Spiritbox, a Canadian heavy metal band from Victoria in British Columbia.

History

Spiritbox was first made by Courtney LaPlante and Mike Stringer who both played and sang in Iwrestledabearonce. In that band, they both felt uncomfortable and that they were not living up to their creative freedom. Courtney LaPlante, Mike Stringer, and Mikey Montgomery left in October 2017. LaPlante and Stringer came together to form Spiritbox, and released their first single together called "The Beauty of Suffering." Mikey helped rearrange and master drum parts on the single. To save money, they collectively decided to not tour until later in their career with this new band.

Bill Crook joined the band as their bassist. Shortly after he joined, they found Ryan Loerke, a talented drummer. He left in 2020. As a temporary replacement, Zen Rosenberg joined. Due to travelling conditions put in place from COVID-19, he never physically joined until they begun their first tour. They met three days in person prior. 

Musical Influences

Spiritbox's musical influences have consisted of Nine Inch Nails, the Cure, Alexisonfire, Protest the Hero, Depeche Mode, Tears for Fears, Gojira, Tesseract, Deftones, Kate Bush, Tool, Meshuggah, and Cannibal Corpse.

Awards and Nominations

  • 2021, They won the award for Best International Breakthrough Band
  • 2021, They were nominated for Best Music Video for the song "Constance"
  • 2020, They won the award for Music Video of the Year for the song "Blessed Be"
